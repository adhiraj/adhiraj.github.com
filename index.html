Sportup!
Matchmaking for people looking for sports players at any time
Mar-Apr '12
A friend of ours was facing a problem – sometimes he wanted to play tennis but couldn't immediately find a partner either because his friends didn't play tennis, their schedules conflicted, or there was no good way of discovering random people interested in playing at the same time. Thus was born Sportup! Just key in your location and see who's playing what in your vicinity, or who's looking for people to play with. You can also schedule future games and friend people on the site (of course, how can a web app be complete without social?). Done using the epic Ruby on Rails framework.
